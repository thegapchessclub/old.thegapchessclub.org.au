Tournaments commenced and were completed close to schedule. The Flood Cup, One Hour and Odd Knights tournaments were submitted for rating with the Australian Chess Federation (ACF) so most players who competed in these tournaments will have an official ACF rating in the December 2007 list. The Allegro tournament was submitted for rapid rating.

 

Major tournaments conducted during 2007 include the following:

  • Flood Cup (including Handicap Trophy)
  • One Hour tournament (including Maiden Trophy)
  • Allegro tournament
  • Lightning tournament.

 

In addition to these I was organiser and arbiter for the 45 Minute, Prize Night, Odd Knights and St David’s Day tournaments. These are the more relaxed of our tournaments, with the aim to make participation fun and enjoyable. Participation in the Odd Knights tournament’s initial year was disappointing. Only 16 games have been played to date, including only 3 games since February 16. Consideration needs to be given as to whether this tournament is retained in the 2008 calendar.

 

The Gap played Samford in a series of interclub matches. These nights were successful, and have been a forum in which players can play serious games without having to be concerned about loss of rating points and tournament standing.

 

In 2008 I foresee a similar tournament calendar to this year, and welcome any suggestions as to how the programme can be improved.

 

Andrew Robinson

Director of Play (DOP)

The Gap Chess Club Inc.
