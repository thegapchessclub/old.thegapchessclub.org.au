AGM 2005 proposals

 

The following four proposals are to be considered by club members at the Annual General Meeting of The Gap Chess Club Inc. which will be held at The Gap State High School on 2 December 2005.

 

Motion 1 – Proposed by Marty Chadwick (Seconded by Andrew Robinson)

It is proposed that for a scoresheet to be published on the club’s website the permission of only one of the players is required.

Note: At present publication can be blocked if one of the players does not give permission to publish.

 

 

Motion 2 – Proposed by Andrew Robinson (Seconded by Debbie Jenkins)

It is proposed that for all games in the Flood Cup and One Hour tournaments, the scheduled commencement time is 8:00pm.

Note: Starting times for games can be varied upon agreement between the players and with the approval of the Director of Play.

 

 

Motion 3 – Proposed by Mark Taylor (Seconded by Andrew Robinson)

It is proposed that a sum of money, the amount to be decided by the committee, be donated to the organisers of the 2006 Australian Championships, and this donation be provided to the organisers in such a way that serves the promotion of The Gap Chess Club.

Note: Mark has specially requested that he be allowed to speak further in support of this motion when it is raised at the AGM

 

Motion 4 – Proposed by Debbie Jenkins (Seconded by Mark Taylor)

 

It is proposed that the club use surplus funds (an amount to be determined by the committee) to purchase books that will be beneficial to the club, and will form the basis of a club library.
