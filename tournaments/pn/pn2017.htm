Method of calculating the starting times for a game

The player with the lower rating receives 5 minutes. The higher rated player receives 5 minute minus the difference in the handicaps of the players, with the proviso that this player will always receive a minimum of one minute on the DGT at the start.

Unrated players would have been given provisional ratings.
