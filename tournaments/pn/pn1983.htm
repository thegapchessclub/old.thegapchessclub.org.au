The Gap Chess Club

Prize Night – circa 1983

 

No tournament was held on prize night back in the early 1980s, however some photos survive of the event which was held at Stuart Wilson’s house.

 

The photos include the presentation of the Flood Cup from then president Nev Cobbold to the winner for that year Garry de Jager.

 

Photos

1. Bruce Petherick (?) in foreground

2. John Guest in the foreground. Behind (l to r) are Garry de Jager, Nev Cobbold and Doug Foster.

3. (l to r) Garry de Jager, Nev Cobbold and Doug Foster.

4. Garry de Jager & Nev Cobbold

5. John Guest in the foreground. Behind (l to r) Nev Cobbold and Stuart Wilson.

6. Nev Cobbold middle; Doug Foster right. Girl to their left unidentified.
