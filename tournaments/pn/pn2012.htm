Handicapping notes:

0.0 – Over 1500

0.5 – 1400 to 1499

1.0 – 1300 to 1399

1.5 – 1200 to 1299

2.0 – 1100 to 1199

3.0 – 1000 to 1099

4.0 – 900 to 999

5.0 – 800 to 899

2.0 – 5min v 3min

2.5 – 5min v 2min&30sec

3.0 – 5min v 2min

3.5 – 5min v 1min&30sec
