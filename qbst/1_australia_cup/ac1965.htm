(0)

1

Wentworth Park, Sydney

After 30 minutes extra time, during which no further goals were scored.

Penalty Kicks: Sydney Hakoah 13 APIA Leichhardt 13, taken in batches of five. After each team had taken 15 penalties, the shootout was abandoned in favour of a replay, due to diminishing light, by mutual agreement of the clubs.
