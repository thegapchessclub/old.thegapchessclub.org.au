Notes on eligibility to enter the Brisbane Cup competition

  • 2003 to 2012 Eligibility limited to clubs from the Brisbane Premier League, Premier Division One and Premier Division Two.
  • 2013 Eligibility limited to clubs from the Brisbane Premier League and the Capital Leagues. Qld National Premier League clubs ineligible.
  • 2014 Qld National Premier League clubs from SE Queensland entered, plus clubs from the Brisbane Premier League and the Capital Leagues. Canale Cup doubled as FFA Cup qualifying.
  • 2015 As occurred in 2014, Qld National Premier League clubs from SE Queensland competed, because the Canale Cup doubled as FFA Cup qualifying, but were ineligible to play in the final
  • 2016 Qld National Premier League clubs ineligible. Ten winners in FFA Cup Round 4 qualifying (prior to entry of Qld National Premier League clubs) progressed to Round 1 of Canale Cup, with six clubs receiving a bye.
