NSL Cup – Table of rankings 1977-1997

 

The table below ranks the clubs participating in the NSL Cup. This competition was the premier knock-out tournament in Australian football prior to the commencement of the FFA Cup in 2014 run by Football Federation Australia.

 

In several editions clubs outside the NSL were allowed to compete:

  • 1979 to 1981
  • 1985 and 1986 (state champions added to the 24 NSL clubs)
  • 1996-97 when several regional/representative teams competed (Northern NSW, South Australian XI and Brisbane Lions)

 

Clubs are listed under their current name, with previous (and sometimes more familiar) names also listed.

 

Code = ‘NSL’ for clubs that ever participated in the NSL; the state is listed for other teams; ‘NNSW’ = Northern NSW; ‘ACT’ = Australian Capital Territory.

 

Eight clubs which joined the NSL after the 1996-97 season are also listed at the bottom of this table.
