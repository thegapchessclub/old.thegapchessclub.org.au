Sources and acknowledgments

 

Key sources used to compile these lists have been:

·        My research into pre-1954 results in the Brisbane Courier-Mail and Sunday Mail from the Trove Newspapers website (this is ongoing)

·        The Football in Australia website (which is encyclopedic)

·        Football Brisbane’s Results Vault

·        The Internet Archive

·        My own records, including soccer annuals from the 1970s and 1980s, soccer programs, and even tables on the backs of old Pools coupons

 

While existing websites contain much of the information on this site, the only online attempt at a Brisbane soccer club database seems to be the OzFootball site.

However while the OzFootball club data is extensive for most other states, the data for Queensland clubs is quite sparse.

 

Many Brisbane soccer club websites (including their Wikipedia pages) reflect a gap in club records beyond the most recent seasons, so this site has been created in part to fill that void.

 

 

All contributions, including correction of errors will be gratefully accepted and acknowledged on this page.
