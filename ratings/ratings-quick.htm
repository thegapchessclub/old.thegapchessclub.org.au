ACF QUICK RATINGS

 

Listed below are the Australian Chess Federation (ACF) quick ratings for players who participated in The Gap Chess Club’s Allegro Tournament since 2006, for the period in which each year’s tournament was rated.

The full ACF Ratings list may be access from the ACF’s Ratings Page.

These ratings were known as the ACF Rapid Ratings up to and including the March 2013 list, after which Blitz tournaments were accepted and ‘Rapid’ became ‘Quick’.
