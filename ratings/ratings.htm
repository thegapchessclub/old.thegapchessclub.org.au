Prior to 1985, rating list were calculated and published periodically, and not according to a fix schedule of releases.

From 1985 to 2002, the ACF calculated and published rating lists in April, August and December (except August 1988).

Since 2003, four lists have been calculated and published each year at the beginning of March, June, September and December.
