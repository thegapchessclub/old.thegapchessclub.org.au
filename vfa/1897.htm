Season Notes

Changes to the membership of the Association

Eight teams left the Association at the end of the 1896 and formed the Victorian Football League.

Brunswick were admitted to the Association in March 1897.

 

Decisions made at the Associationâ€™s AGM held on Friday 9 April 1897

1. Behinds would now count towards the score. A goal would count for six points, a behind for one point.

2. Team size was reduced from 20 to 18 men.

3. Points for the premiership would be counted as four points for a win, two points for a draw.

 

North Melbourne home matches

The North Melbourne Cricket Ground was under an upgrade during the winter of 1897

The overcome this, the North Melbourne Football Club arranged to ground share with Essendon FC and played most of their home matches at the EMCG
