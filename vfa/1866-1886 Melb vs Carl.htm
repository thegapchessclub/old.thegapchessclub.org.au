Melbourne vs Carlton matches – 1866 to 1886

 

Background

Melbourne (formed 1858) and Carlton (1864) first played each other in 1866.

They played each other three time each season from 1866-1869, then four times every season from 1870 to 1883.

During the 1870s the pre-eminence of these clubs meant their clashes decided Victoria’s premier club in every season from 1871 to 1877.

Only the emergence of Geelong in 1878 saw the breaking of the Melbourne-Carlton domination.

 

It was not until 1886 that Melbourne and Carlton played each other just twice in a season, regularising this clash among the other fixtures.
