Notes on the Final Ladder

At a meeting of the Association held on Monday 12 July 1915 it was decided to curtail the season after Round 13 played on 17 July 1915,
and to commence the Final Series the following Saturday.
This decision was formally ratified at a Special General Meeting held on Wednesday 14 July 1915.
