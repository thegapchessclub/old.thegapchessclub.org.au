Report – 25 March 2005

 

The club was closed for Good Friday.

 

Flood Cup

 

One postponed game was played on Easter Monday (25 March).

 

Round 2 result:

Phillip Kirkman 1 Ray Adams 0

 

Ray’s account:

Phillip and I played round 2 last night.  I lost, but it was an interesting game of tactics.  Despite missing the best line in the opening and blundering a pawn, due to inaccurate play on Phillip's part, I managed to enter the middle game with many tactical threats bestowed by a pair of advanced knights that were radiating power and pressure into his postion.  I was given chances to gain a piece up, but failed to see these winning lines.  Consequently Phillip finally came through with a won rook and pawn endgame.  This probably shows that I should concentrate my training on raw middle game analysis and correct execution of the combinations.
