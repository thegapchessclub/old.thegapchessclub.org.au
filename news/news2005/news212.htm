Round 10 results:

Tim Johnson 1 Mark Taylor 1 (Tim won first game on Aug 26; Mark won tonight’s game)

Andrew Robinson 2 Peter Hiller 0

 

The first two rounds of the Allegro Tournament were played tonight. Most games in the first round went to form, with the closest fixture being Marty v Garvin. Marty won the first game with black then lost with white. He did likewise in the second round against Tim, thus reversing his good form with white on the opening night.

 

Peter and Mick scored one each, with Peter emphasising his victory in game two with a thunderous crash of the queen on the board with the checkmate delivering move. Mark gained a deserved draw off Ray, and later comfortably won the second half of his fixture with Tim. Andrew won all four of his games tonight to move to the top of the standings.
