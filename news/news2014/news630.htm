Report – 6 June 2014

 

Flood Cup

 

Round 6 result (Final match):

Andrew Mather 1 Russell Lyons 0

 

Andrew Mather won this game comfortably within an hour to seal his first Flood Cup triumph.

Russell’s loss meant Marcus Porter won the Handicap Trophy section of this tournament.

Stuart Wilson Trophy

 

Round 1 results:

Marcus Porter 1 Jim Petrie 0

Peter Hiller 0 Martin Laizans 1

John Ryland 0 Andrew Robinson 1

James Hiller ½ Warren Mayocchi ½

 

Peter and John both fought out well before losing their games.

 

Peter was in fact well in front against Martin, and his position only disintegrated in the final moves.

 

James was behind in the opening, but recovered well to be a pawn and the exchange up in a queen and rook endgame. With just 15 minutes left of his time, and playing for the first time in several months, James erred on the side of caution and offered his opponent a draw which was readily accepted.

 

Jim’s game was the first to finish on the night, with Marcus commencing his campaign with a win.
