Report – 13 February 2004

 

Flood Cup

 

Results:

Peter Hiller 0 Andrew Robinson 1

George Flitcroft-Smith 1 John Curran 0

Stuart Wilson 0 Harvey Quaresmini 1

Ray Adams 1 James Hiller 0

Marty Chadwick ½ Garvin Gray ½

Debbie Jenkins 0 Stuart Holt 1

Steve Quick 1 Brian Willcock 0

 

Most games went to plan as is supposed to be the case in the first round of a Swiss. The most interesting games involved the three new players with Stuart Holt and Steve Quick both scoring victories while Garvin Gray was unable to press his advantage (R+2P v R) in the endgame against Marty. The Chadwick-Gray game was the last game to finish for the evening.

 

There were mixed reactions to the new time controls and it will be interesting to see how these will affect later rounds. It was noted that several players had more time than they commenced with at the end of the game.

 

Two players arrived late and were therefore at a time disadvantage at the start. Peter was 40 minutes late while Harvey was 55 minutes late (just 5 minutes short of a forfeit). Players are reminded to be at the club on or before 8.30pm on match nights. This should be normal courtesy to your opponent. Due to the extended time controls for this tournament the DOP will be strictly enforcing the start time for games, and docking time for the latecomers.

 

Mark Wardle has been replaced in the tournament by new member Stefan Harmat. Tony Howes will now play his first round match against Stefan on 20 February.
