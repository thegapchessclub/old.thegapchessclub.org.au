Report – 3 December 2004

 

Annual General Meeting

There were few radical changes resulting from this year’s AGM. The meeting ran fairly smoothly with issues discussed and everyone having a chance to have their say.

 

The main outcomes of the meeting were:

·         The club will now meet at 7.30pm (previously 8pm)

·         Tournament matches will start at 8pm (previously 8.30pm)

·         The new One Hour time control will be 60min + 10 sec

·         In future, a scoresheet will not be published on this website if either player does not wish it to be published.

·         Club fee structure for 2005 will remain as it was in 2004

·         Minor changes to the rules for eligibility for the Maiden Trophy.
