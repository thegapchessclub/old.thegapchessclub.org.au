Report – 10 September 2004

 

One Hour tournament

 

Round 12 results:

Debbie Jenkins 1 George Flitcroft-Smith 0

Andrew Robinson ½ Luthien Russell ½

 

Round 13 result:

Marty Chadwick 0 Brian Willcock 1

 

Three games were played, the results of which make for a very interesting final round. George misjudged a combination against Debbie and lost a rook, after which the fight to win was fairly hopeless. Luthien pulled a brilliant move out of the hat at move 19 that caused Andrew to spend 16 minutes on his next move and gave Luthien the initiative. Luthien eventually accepted a draw offer from Andrew who realised what was going on in the other game.

 

Brian and Marty brought forward their game from next week and in the end Brian won a tight contest that could have gone either way. The James Hiller v Stuart Holt game has been postponed to Sept 24.

 

There were three visitors tonight: Dusan Smrek, who has been around recently; Adam Lucas, a Gap local who is coming back to the game after a long lapse; and Terry Krause from the Suncoast club. Of these, the first two have expressed interest in entering the Allegro tournament which starts on September 24.
