Interclub v Samford Valley Chess Club

 

The Gap ended the night undefeated with three wins and three draws. All games were played with a one hour guillotine time control.

Flood Cup

 

Results (Round 7):

Josh Ford 1 Nathan Shaw 0

 

The first game of the night to finish was the Flood Cup game which was all over by 9pm. Josh won his first game of the tournament ensuring that every player that finished the tournament won at least one game.
