Report – 28 May 2010

 

Flood Cup

 

Round 7 results:

Andrew Robinson 0 Nathan Shaw 1

Cameron De Vere 1 Jim Petrie 0

 

Cameron won his game within 30 minutes. Nathan was made to work much harder and needed 71 moves to win his game.

 

Nathan’s win has won him the Handicap Trophy for 2010.

 

A play-off game for the Flood Cup will occur next Friday night between Nathan and Cameron.
