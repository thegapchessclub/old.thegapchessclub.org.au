Report - 1 October 2010

 

Stuart Wilson Trophy

 

Results:

Jim Petrie 1 Jonathan Wilson 0 (Round 13)

Jonathan Wilson 0 James Hiller 1 (Round 4)

 

Jon lost his game to Jim, after playing well at first, but caving in towards the end.

 

This early finish allowed time for Jon to play James in a long awaited clash.

Jon had actually been awarded the game on forfeit months ago, but preferred to risk his free point by playing the game over the board.

Jon had a strong early advantage but lost his queen to a knight fork which levelled the game.

James had a roving queen for two unconnected rooks, and his better position allowed him a clear win at the end.

 

Unplayed games in this tournament

The eight unplayed games in this tournament can still be played up until October 29.

If both players agree, a game awarded on forfeit (there are five after tonight) can be played over the board, with the actual result replacing the forfeit result in the final table.
