Report – 4 February 2011

 

Lightning Tournament

 

After the first night of play it seems one of the three front runners will take the Lightning Crown for 2011.

 

Other played well, although Allegro runner-up Jim Petrie saying that the time control was a little too fast for his liking.

 

Promising junior Marcus Porter came for his first night at the club and put in a creditable performance.
