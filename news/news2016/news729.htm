Report – 12 August 2016

 

Stuart Wilson Trophy

 

Round 7 results:

Andrew Mather 1 Brian Willcock 0

Andrew Robinson ½ Tony Howes ½

Tom Meggitt 0 Tony Morris 1

Daniel Clancy 0 Martin Laizans 1

Adam Morris 0 Daniel Sheldon 1

Ray Adams v James Hiller – postponed to August 20

John Ryland 1 Edwin Mejia Ortiz 0 – on forfeit

Nick Petrie v Peter Hiller – postponed to August 20

 

Andrew Mather completed a picket fence and Tony Morris claimed second place in the final round’s play.

 

Andrew Robinson had a chance to catch Tony Morris, but ended up with a third draw this year against Tony Howes in a more adventurous game than their past two meetings.

 

Other winners were Martin Laizans and Daniel Sheldon who won their final round games to finish on a respectable four points from the seven rounds.

 

The Tom Meggitt – Tony Morris game can be viewed here: https://en.lichess.org/pQOtY7mU
