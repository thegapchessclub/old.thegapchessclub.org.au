Report – 20 May 2016

 

Flood Cup

 

Round 7 results:

Gary Sands 0 Tony Howes 1 (played May 13)

Andrew Robinson 1 Joshua Morris 0

Tony Morris ½ Martin Laizans ½

Ray Adams 1 Peter Hiller 0

Daniel Sheldon 1 Brian Willcock 0 (played May 13)

Joe Curtain v Adam Morris – to be played May 27

Cameron De Vere 1 Daniel Clancy 0

John Ryland 1 James Hiller 0

Peter Black – half point BYE

 

Two games were pivotal in deciding the outcome of this year’s Flood Cup, with three players capable of achieving five points and forcing a play-off with Tony Howes.

 

Both Andrew and Joshua were on four points going into the game, so with a draw useless to both players, an asymmetric attacking game ensued.

Joshua sacrificed a pawn for a kingside attack that sought a bishop-queen checkmate in the corner.

Andrew moved to avoid this and found his queen attacked by a rook protected by a bishop.

After the queen had been exchanged for rook and bishop, Andrew had neutralised Joshua other bishop and rook, and mate followed in the corner.

 

Martin had much the better of a draw with Tony Morris, who was never really a chance of gaining the win he needed to join the play-off series.

 

Ray Adams and Cameron De Vere had comfortable victories while John Ryland completed an impressive finish to the tournament securing a second consecutive win.
