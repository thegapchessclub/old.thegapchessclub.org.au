Report – 27 May 2016

 

Flood Cup

 

Round 7 results:

Joe Curtain 0 Adam Morris 1 (on forfeit)

 

Play-Off Match for First Place:

Andrew Robinson ½ Tony Howes ½

 

 

The play-off match proved to be a rather dull affair after a pawn blockade developed which stifled any attacking possibilities.

Tony ran the game in a program from the position at Move 19 when the draw was agreed, and the computer came up with a 180 move draw!
