Report – 16 May 2003

 

Flood Cup

 

Results:

Eric Faccer 0 Tony Howes 1

Mike Talbot 0 Harvey Quaresmini 1

 

Just two games were played tonight. In the crucial game Eric needed a draw to win the tournament, while a win would see him achieve the first Flood Cup picket fence since Peter Cronin in 1986. Eric dominated the middle stages being a crucial tempo ahead. However a missed rook move allowed Tony to grab the initiative and win a very open and interesting game.

 

George is the only one who can catch Eric. A win next week against Ray Adams will see a single game play-off for the Flood Cup occurring on May 30.
