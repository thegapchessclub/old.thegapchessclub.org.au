Report – Tuesday, 5 August 2003

 

Interclub v Samford Valley Chess Club

Samford Valley Chess Club hosted The Gap Chess Club in the return fixture for the match played at The Gap on April 4 (see news item 85). Match-ups were drawn at random as is the local custom. All games were close and The Gap was fortunate to win 3½-2½. Gabby swapped sides and played for The Gap. All games started at 7.30pm, so Harvey unfortunately missed a game when showing up at 8pm. However there were many social games to be had, and an enjoyable night was had by all. All games were played with a 60 minutes guillotine finish.
