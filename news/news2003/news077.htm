Report – 7 February 2003

 

Lightning Tournament

 

Only one match-up played tonight, and this started at 12 midnight, and finished around 12.40am. The play was accordingly dreadful with queens being left en pris 4 times (at least) in 4 games. James has found other interests on Friday night (at least for the next few weeks) and will not take further part in this tournament. Her name is Shelley.

 

Next week should be the final week of lightning play (Andrew v Paul & Andrew v Gabriel remain). It is expected that the Flood Cup will start on Feb 21. All entries should contact the club by Feb 14 to confirm entry.

 

Result:

Andrew 3 Harvey 1
