Report – 25 April 2003

 

Flood Cup

 

Results:

Peter Hiller 0 Andrew Robinson 1

Tony Howes 1 Gabriel Boross 0

Aaron Michaux 0 Brian Willcock 1 (awarded on forfeit)

Mike Talbot 0 Debbie Jenkins 1

 

Three games were played over the board tonight and the other was decided on forfeit following the mysterious non-appearance of Aaron (SARS-related?).

 

Andrew added to his jetlag (in from Japan this morning) by winning a long drawn out game with Peter that finished around midnight. Tony quickly accounted for Gabby while Mike and Debbie exchanged pieces helter skelter until Debbie had only a rook left. Mike being unfamiliar with the K+RvK ending fought it out to the bitter end and received mate over the board.

 

Play on the night was continued by a few hardened fanatics until 1.20am.
