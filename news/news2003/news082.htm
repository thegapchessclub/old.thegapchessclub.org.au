Report – 14 March 2003

 

Flood Cup

 

Results:

Tony Howes 1 Andrew Robinson 0

Harvey Quaresmini 1 Eric Faccer 0 (forfeit)

Brian Willcock 1 John Curran 0

Mike Talbot 0 Peter Hiller 1

Debbie Jenkins 1 James Hiller 0

 

Four games were played over the board tonight, with Tony showing he will be among those to beat by defeating the reigning Flood Cup champion. George and Paul agreed to play next week, while Eric failed to appear tonight (the scheduled night) so Harvey was awarded the game.
