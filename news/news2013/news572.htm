Report – 22 February 2013

 

Lightning Tournament

 

The tournament came down to the Round 7 clash between Andrew Mather and George Flitcroft-Smith, with Andrew winning a tense match.

 

A win by Andrew Robinson over George had set up the final head-to-head play-off. George also conceded a draw to Marcus which meant there was no play-off for first place, despite Andrew Mather’s late upset loss to Martin Laizans.
