Allegro Tournament

 

Results:

Brian Thomas 2 Mark Taylor 0

Gordon Powell 1 Mark Taylor 1

Greg Colwell 1 John Curran 1

 

Only three tournament games were played tonight under the direction of acting DOP Mark Taylor, who was also a participant in both games. Brian won twice to round off a 100% tournament for him, while Mark grabbed a vital point off Gordon in the close scramble for positions from 7th to 12th place.

 

James and Peter Hiller put in a rare attendance tonight as did new member Cecil and a few others on a quiet night for the club.
