Report – 12 May 2006

 

Flood Cup

 

Round 6 result:

Brian Thomas 1 Phillip Kirkman 0

 

Round 7 results:

Garvin Gray 0 Tony Howes 1

Russell Lyons 0 Cameron De Vere 1

Mark Taylor 0 Kieran Lyons 1

 

Brian Thomas won against Phil Kirkman meaning that he only needs a draw in his final game to win the Flood Cup. Tony won his final round game against Garvin to keep alive his chance of a play-off match should Brian falter in Round 7.

 

Kieran and Cameron won to join the traffic jam of players on four points.
