Report – 3 May 2002

 

The monthly junior tournament was held tonight with 14 players competing in Groups A & B.  Kieran Lyons and newcomer Rishabh Bhindari won their respective divisions, so congratulations go to these players, but also to the other players who all had their turn at putting in a good performance.
