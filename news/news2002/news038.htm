Report – 31 May 2002 – 3 June 2002

 

Junior Tournament

A junior tournament was held tonight (31 May).  12 entries played in 3 groups of 4 players.

 

Summary of the results:

Group A: Chris Yu 3, Phillip Jones 2, Anthony Ji ½, Rishabh Bhandari ½

Group B: Sean Purdon 2, Matthew Cannon 1½, Thomas Robinson 1½, Chris Rowe 1

Group C: Morgan Groth 3, Jacob Gatt 2, Thomas Mellor 1, Benjamin Cox 0

 

It should be mentioned that Morgan and Benjamin were first nighters, and that Benjamin won a replay of one of his games, thereby ensuring that no one lost all of their games on the night.

 

Full Results of Junior Tournament

 

 

Interclub

The Gap club won all 5 games against the visiting side comprising of old club stalwart Ray Adams and 4 of his friends from the BCF.  Despite the one-sidedness of the result, a whitewash it certainly was not.  All games were hotly contested and a few went right down to the wire.  Time controls were 90 minutes on the clock to make all moves.

Flood Cup 2002

 

Result:

Paul Evans ½ Andrew Robinson ½

 

This game was played midweek (3 June 2002).  The result saw Andrew win the Flood Cup for the third time, and retain the title he won last year. The draw also ensured that Paul would achieve his highest final position in the Flood Cup (second).

45 Minute tournament 2002 results in detail

 

 

Transfer

A few games were played with James having an amazing run of success, unparalleled in anything that had gone before.  At one stage he was involved in 4 successful pairings.  Two of the BCF visitors, Luke and Brendan (a transfer novice) stayed for a few transfer games, and everyone had a thoroughly enjoyable time.  While the results were shared around and everyone had a few wins, James seemed be involved in a disproportional number of winning pairings.  Has the tide turned??
