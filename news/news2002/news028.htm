Report – 22 March 2002

 

Flood Cup 2002

 

Results:

Paul Evans 1 Stuart Wilson 0

James Hiller 0 Peter Hiller 1

Brian Willcock 0 George Flitcroft-Smith 1

Doug Foster 1 Gabriel Boross 0

 

On the night that saw the return of 1980s club stalwart Ray Adams, Paul Evans’ perfect run continued, and Doug pulled off a brilliant victory. Brian with his Sammy-look-alike beard, James (cricket tragic extraordinaire) and Stuart also maintained their perfect records (alas!).

45 Minute Tournament

 

Results:

Ben Lazarus 0 George Flitcroft-Smith 1

Nathaneal Lim 1 Harvey Quaresmini 0

 

Defending champion Harvey Quaresmini played his first game of this tournament and got off to a losing start against junior Nathaneal Lim. In the other game George eventually triumphed against Ben with K+RvK at the end. Ben had exhibited a "Yu-ism" during the game, in that he had asked George if he wanted to resign as his Flood Cup opponent had just shown up!
