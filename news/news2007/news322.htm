Report – 23 November 2007

 

45 Minute Tournament

 

Results:

Brian Willcock 0 Russell Lyons 1

Kieran Lyons 1 Ray Adams 0

Nathan Shaw 1 Ray Adams 0

 

Brian resigned after he touched his queen not realising that Russell’s bishop had checked his king, and his queen had a suicidal legal move to block the check.

 

Kieran beat Ray in a fairly complicated game, while Nathan managed to get an important pawn advantage in a pawn endgame. This win means Nathan should win Group B and enter a play-off with Group A winner Andrew.
