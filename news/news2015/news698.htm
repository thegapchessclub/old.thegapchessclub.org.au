Allegro Tournament

 

Only three players were in attendance at a busy time of the year, so no Allegro games were played on the final scheduled night for play.

 

Therefore the table is finalised.

 

Unplayed matches involving players ranked 12 to 14 have been awarded 1-0 to their opponents.

A 1-0 score has been awarded instead of 2-0 so as to not disadvantage players who have played their games and risked a loss, and to act as an incentive to completing fixtures in future tournaments.

 

Three games not involving players 12 to 14 have had no points awarded.
