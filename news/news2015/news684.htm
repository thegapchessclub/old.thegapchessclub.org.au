Report – 14 August 2015

 

Stuart Wilson Trophy

 

Round 6 results:

Tony Morris 0 Ray Adams 1

Martin Laizans 1 Tony Howes 0  

 

Two games were played tonight, with Martin Laizans achieving his first long-form tournament win over Tony Howes.

Ray Adams won his game to remain in contention.

 

If Ray Adams and Andrew Robinson both win their final round games against the top two, there will be a four player, or perhaps five player play-off to decide the tournament.

 

Similarly, if Joe Curtain wins against Joshua Morris in Round Seven, there will be a multi-player play-off to decide the Maiden Trophy.
