Stuart Wilson Trophy

 

Round 7 results:

Ray Adams 0 Andrew Mather 1 (played August 21)

Cameron De Vere 1 Andrew Robinson 0 (played August 21)

Joshua Morris 1 Joe Curtain 0

Stefan Flanigan 0 Martin Laizans 1 (on forfeit)

Metin Ozaslan 1 Adam Morris 0

John Ryland 0 Nathan Key 1

Brian Willcock 1 Jim Petrie 0

Tony Howes 1 Nick Petrie 0 (played August 21)

Tony Morris ½ point BYE

 

Play-Off Match for First Place:

Andrew Mather ½ Cameron De Vere ½

Outcome: Title shared

 

The first play-off for five years in a classical rated tournament at The Gap CC saw a repeat of the previous result with a draw and a shared title, as occurred in 2010.

The unusualness of the five year break is shown in this list of play-offs in classically rated tournaments at The Gap Chess Club.

 

Cameron and Andrew played an even game which meant the Stuart Wilson Trophy title for 2015 is shared by these two players.

 

If Joshua Morris had lost his game, there would have been an unprecedented five-way play-off (between Joshua, Joe, Tony M, Nathan and Metin).

Fortunately for the sake of scheduling, Joshua won in a close game where he was the exchange down (R+R vs R+B) but made the most of his compensatory pawns.

 

Metin, Nathan and Brian all won their final round games, while Martin was the beneficiary of a free point which elevated him to fourth position in the final standings.
