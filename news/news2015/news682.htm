Stuart Wilson Trophy

 

Round 5 results:

Jim Petrie 0 Nick Petrie 1

Andrew Mather won against Tony Howes to move clear at the top of the standings after Cameron was held to a draw by Tony Morris.

 

Andrew had a frustrating night, having a small advantage against Ray, but eventually having to settle for a draw.

 

Metin and Joe had good wins to move up to three points, while Adam Morris showed his win over Brian was no fluke with a further win over John.

 

James held out for a while, but eventually Stefan was able to notch his second win of this tournament.
