Report – 10 August 2001

 

One Hour Tournament 2001

 

Results:

Gabriel Boross 1 Doug Foster 0

Patrick Tao 0 Harvey Quaresmini 1

Brian Willcock 1 Stuart Wilson 0

 

Three games were played this week. Gabriel Boross and Brian Willcock both won to maintain their challenge for the One Hour title. Harvey pulled off a comeback from the dead to win his game against Patrick who was taotaolly bemused by a discovered attack on the queen with check.
