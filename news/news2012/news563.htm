Report – 30 November 2012

 

Interclub Match vs Bullwinkle Chess Club

 

Bullwinkle won on four boards (plus our loan player Shane Mathers) to secure a comprehensive victory.

 

The Gap’s best moment came with an upset victory by Andrew Mather whose contests with Michael Van Pelt are developing a nice rivalry.

 

Tony Howes also performed well above his rating to secure an impressive draw against a 2000+ rated player.
