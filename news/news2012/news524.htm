Report – 2 March 2012

 

Flood Cup

 

The Flood Cup commenced tonight with just one early game played:

 

Round 5 result:

Martin Laizans 1 Ray Adams 0

 

Martin caught Ray out with his own French Defence and when Ray missed the rply to 9. a3 his game was on the back foot ever after.

 

Nine players have entered this year’s Flood Cup and its format will be Round-Robin.
