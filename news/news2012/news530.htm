Report – 20 April 2012

 

Flood Cup

 

Round 6 results:

Greg Colwell 1 Martin Laizans 0

Marcus Porter v Brian Willcock – Postponed

Tony Howes 0 Andrew Robinson 1 (on forfeit)

John Ryland v Jim Petrie – Postponed

Ray Adams – BYE

 

Other results:

John Ryland 0 Ray Adams 1 (from Round 1)

Brian Willcock 1 Jim Petrie 0 (from Round 9)

 

Three games were played over the board tonight.

Greg played one of his best games to beat Martin.

Brian gave away a piece early but then played with Tal-like brilliance to force a win.

John lost a knight early and was unable to recover the deficit against the methodical play of Ray.

In addition to these games, Tony advised he was unable to attend and conceded his game.
