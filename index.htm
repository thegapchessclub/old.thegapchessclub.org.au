The Gap Chess Club meets:

·        every Friday night at 7.45pm

·        at The Gap State High School, B Block (Room B6), Waterworks Road, The Gap.

The Gap is 10 km due west of Brisbane CBD.

 

The club conducts regular chess tournaments. See our Tournament Schedule 2018 for details.

 

Membership Application Form 2018

 

Club Membership for the 2018 calendar year is $10 (Juniors $5).

 

CAQ membership for 2018 is $20. To play in our rated tournaments there is a requirement that you are a member of the CAQ (Chess Association of Queensland).

 

Tournament entry for all 2018 tournaments is $5. There is no discount for entering a single tournament.

 

Night fees for the 2018 calendar year are $4 ($5 non-members); $2 for Juniors ($3 Junior non-members)

 

 

The Gap Chess Club is affiliated with the Chess Association of Queensland (CAQ).
