Queensland Chess 1975 (includes Singer Cup)

 

 

The following article appeared in Chess in Australia  (August 1975, page 192).

 

SINGER CUP INTER-CLUB COMPETITION

Progress scores in the Singer Cup Inter-club team’s Championship are:

 

Division A – Brisbane 10-4; The Gap 8x-4; Q.I.T. 8-4; Annerley 6½-4; Redcliffe 6½-4

Division B – Brisbane (2) 19-6; Brisbane (3) 13-6; Q.I.T. (2) 11½-6; University 11x-6; Pine Rivers 10-6; The Gap (2) 9½-6; Annerley (2) 9-6

Division C – Brisbane Prison 16-6; Inala 13½-4; Annerley 12½-5; Library 11½-6; Pine Rivers (2) 7-5; Q.I.T. (3) 6½-4; Brisbane (4) 5-6.

(The second figure indicates the number of matches played. Severla results still outstanding).

 

 

The following article appeared in Chess in Australia  (October 1975, page 240).

 

SINGER CUP INTER-CLUB COMPETITION

Progress scores in this year’s Metropolitan inter-club teams’ Championship are:

 

Division A – Brisbane 19-7; Annerley 13½-8; The Gap 12½-6; Q.I.T. 11x-6; Redcliffe 11-7

Division B – Brisbane (2) 25½-9; Brisbane (3) 23x-10; Q.I.T. (2) 22-10; University 21½x-10; Pine Rivers 19½-10; The Gap (2) 14-10; Annerley (2) 13½-11

Division C – Inala 30½-10; Annerley 22½-10; Library 22½-10; B.P.C.C. (Brisbane Prison) 19½-8; Brisbane (4) 16½-11; Q.I.T. (3) 13-9; Pine Rivers (2) 11½-10.

 

All divisions are now near completion, with only eight matches remaining in Division A and 12 in each of the other sections.

 

Alan Thomas

 

 

The following article appeared in Chess in Australia  (December 1975, page 281).

 

BRISBANE CLUB CHAMPIONSHIP

Progress scores after four rounds (leaders only) are:

A. Truscott (State Champion), M. Cashman and C. Morris 3½; A. Pardoen, A. Kavetsky and S. Bogdanov 3; M. Campbell 2½.

 

 

SINGER CUP INTER-CLUB COMPETITION

The Brisbane club took top honours in this year’s teams’ competition, with a convincing win in the “A’ division, and a monopoly of the “B” division.

 

FINAL SCORES

Division A – Brisbane 22; The Gap 15½; Annerley 14½; Redcliffe 14; Q.I.T. 14

Division B – Brisbane (2) 34; Brisbane (3) 28½; University 26; Q.I.T. (2) 25; Pine Rivers 22; The Gap (2) 17½; Annerley (2) 15

Division C –Inala 35; BPCC. (Brisbane Prison) 31½; Library 27; Annerley 25½; Brisbane (4) 19; Q.I.T. (3) 17½; Pine Rivers (2) 11½.
