Joe Tarnawski Trophy

 

Joe Tarnawski donated this trophy to the club in 1993 and it has been awarded every subsequent year to the player with the best achievement in the combined Allegro and Lightning tournaments. A formula is used to calculate the winner based on the percentage of games won in each tournament.
