Points system used in 1985

15 points for every point gained in the Flood Cup

10 points for every point gained in the Handicap

1 point for every point gained in the Allegro

1 point for every point gained in the Lightning

 

To be eligible for a placing in the BAR a player must have completed at least half the rounds in ALL FOUR TOURNAMENTS
